prep time

15 min.

cook time

50-55 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Peel, wash and grate all the carrots. Take a wide pan and heat about 4 tsps of ghee. To this, add the finely grated carrots and saute on low flame for 20 mins.

step 2

Now, add a cup of milk, sugar and cardamom powder to the carrots. Saute and further cook slowly for another 30 minutes while stirring every few minutes until it changes to a darker orange/brownish color.

step 3

In another pan, take a tsp of ghee, fry the cashews and raisins and add these to the halwa.

step 3

Serve just as a dessert or along side some plain vanilla ice-cream.

ingredients

Gajar/Carrots 2 lbs

Sugar, Ghee, Cashews, Golden Rasisins

1 cup skim milk
